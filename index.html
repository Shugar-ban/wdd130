Osun State, Nigeria

Osun state is a state in the south western part of Nigeria and it is majorly dominated by the Yoruba people. It is bordered by Kwara state to the north, Oyo state to the west, Ogun state to the south, Ondo and ekiti states to the east.It's a landlocked state known for its rich cultural heritage and the famous Osun-Osogbo cultural festival
